# Following the blog (RSS)

New posts go up as the course is built in public. The lowest-friction way to catch them — no account, no algorithm, no inbox clutter — is **RSS**.

## What's RSS?

RSS is a simple *feed* a site publishes. You subscribe once in a **reader app**, and new posts show up there automatically, newest first. It's just for *you* — no login, no tracking, nothing in your email.

## How to follow

1. **Pick a reader app** (any of these work — there are many more):
    - **[Feedly](https://feedly.com)** — web + mobile, free tier
    - **[NetNewsWire](https://netnewswire.com)** — free, open-source (Mac / iOS)
    - **Thunderbird** — if you already use it for email, it reads feeds too
2. **Add a feed URL:**
    - New posts: `https://dakotalearns.com/feed_rss_created.xml`
    - Updated posts: `https://dakotalearns.com/feed_rss_updated.xml`
3. That's it — new (or updated) posts will appear in your reader.

Prefer to just check in now and then? The [blog](../blog/index.md) is always here, no subscription needed.
