# Dakota Learns

**Free, beginner-friendly computer science — taught in public.**

No prior experience assumed. No paywall. Just clear, honest material that takes you from "I've never written code" to real programming skills — built out loud, one course at a time.

## What you'll find here

- **Courses** — full, university-aligned CS courses you can learn from for free. First up: **CS 1050 — Computer Science 1 (Python)**.
- **Guides** — standalone walk-throughs for common starting points (installing Python, choosing an editor).
- **Blog** — the build-in-public journal: what I'm making, why, and what I'm learning teaching it.

## Why free

I believe the fundamentals of computing should be accessible to anyone willing to learn. Everything here is free to read and learn from. If it helps you and you want to support more of it, there's a way to — but you'll never hit a wall.

## Who makes this

I'm Dakota — a data engineer who learned on free and open resources and wants to pay that forward while preparing to teach. [More about the project :material-arrow-right:](about.md)
