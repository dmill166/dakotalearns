# About

## The project

**Dakota Learns** is an effort to make the fundamentals of computer science genuinely accessible — free, beginner-first, and built in the open. The courses here are designed to be portable (recognizable as solid CS anywhere), aligned to recognized standards (ABET, ACM/IEEE-CS CS2023), and honest about what they do and don't cover.

It's also built *in public*: the materials live in open repositories, and the blog documents the process of creating and teaching them.

## Who

I'm Dakota Hollmann — a data engineer (MBA in Data Analytics, BS in Computer Science) preparing to teach introductory CS. I learned largely on free and open resources, and this is me paying that forward.
